Born in MP and raised in Jaipur, S P Bharill always dreamt big, despite his humble beginnings. He shifted several careers (from diamond tools business to media and entertainment), in the pursuit of finding his calling and also in the spirit of grabbing every opportunity that came his way until 1999. That year, he got introduced to the Direct Selling Market, which marked itself as the pivot to his exponential growth.

What drew me to the concept of the Direct Selling market is not just the moneymaking aspect of it - but it’s inherent ability to multiply time and manpower! The idea of my growth rooted in the growth of a community sparked my love for its holistic nature. It’s been several decades and I haven’t spent a single day without helping myself and a community grow to be better human beings and dare to dream a life of luxury and fulfilment!”
